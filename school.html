WELCOME TO SHRI SIDDHARAMESHWAR PU COLLEGE

Shri siddharameshwar Pre University College started its historic journey in 1998 with the vision of moulding a better tomorrow. Today, Shri siddharameshwar has not only become a household name, but also a synonym for excellence, expertise, eminence and efficiency.

Shri siddharameshwar P.U College has stood out to be a very special educational institution having carved a niche for itself in a short span.

In the course of fulfilling its vision, the college has made remarkable achievements and emerged as one of the premier institutions of the nation.
